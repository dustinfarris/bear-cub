* Design

Verbatim from DESIGN.org for this story's scope.

** Kiosk banner (=KioskLive=, per kid =<header>=)

The kid-color banner is the standing home of child identity and now carries the points readout and the completion icon:

- *Points badge* — a persistent per-kid badge in the banner, tied to child identity (child-color semantics), *always visible* regardless of routine state. Displays =max(0, total)= (never negative, SC-4). It belongs to the child, not the routine, so it is independent of the routine card's collapse/expand and of the header-bar retirement. (SC-1, D43)

** Live updates (context this design builds on, in scope)

*Live updates*: =Phoenix.PubSub= topic ="chores"=; every =Chores= write broadcasts a payload-free =:chores_changed=; subscribers re-fetch (never patch from payload). A derived points total recomputes for free on that existing re-fetch — no new broadcast plumbing.

** Context (=BearCub.Chores=) — in scope

- The kiosk reads the total in =load/2= and recomputes for free on the existing =:chores_changed= re-fetch.

** Decision Log entry in scope

- *D43 (2026-07-16) — Persistent per-kid points badge in the banner:* the always-visible points readout lives in the kid-color banner tied to child identity, showing =max(0, total)=, independent of the routine card's collapse/expand and of the header-bar retirement because it belongs to the child, not the routine. Rejected — a completion-only or routine-scoped indicator: SC-1 requires the total be always visible, so it cannot live on the routine card that collapses. Settles SC-1 placement. → Screens (banner), Key Behaviors.

* User Story

As a kid, I need my points total always showing in my banner next to my name, so that I can see my growing reward at any time — during the routine, after I finish, or at night — without tapping anything.

* Technical Notes

The Design excerpt above is the source of truth; DESIGN.org is broader context.

- Depends on Story 02 (the floored per-kid total) and Story 01 (the columns it derives from). This is the first story that puts a real, growing points figure on the kiosk — the early-visibility milestone (SC-1) before any fail or celebration UI exists.
- Read the total in =load/2= per kid and assign it; the badge is plain server-rendered markup in the banner =<header>=, not a LiveComponent (the kiosk renders server-side). The existing ="chores"= =:chores_changed= re-fetch recomputes it for free — do not add a broadcast.
- Give the badge a stable DOM id per kid (e.g. =points-badge-#{kid.id}=) so LiveView tests can assert the number via =element/2=; test against the element, not raw HTML text.
- The completion icon and its bonus badge are a *different* affordance landed in Story 08; this story adds only the always-visible per-child total. The badge here must remain visible even after Story 08 introduces the completion icon (it belongs to the child, not the routine).
- Floor is already applied by the Story 02 total; the badge just displays it. Include a test with a below-zero signed standing to prove the displayed value is 0.
